Target Audience


The target audience for localweatherstation.com is described below:

  • Who: Farmers, commnity leaders, truck drivers, event planners, and scientists. Plus anyone else who wonders if they are going to get rained on.
  • Age: 5 to 100
  • Income: All income levels. Monthly Subscription is only $10 per month
  • Motivation: To provide and easy to use and accuatre application that allows people to plan their lives around Mother Nature's whims.
