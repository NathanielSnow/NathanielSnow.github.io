Site Purpose

To provide professial, constantly updated weather information to the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven for the use of individuals and businesses. The hompage contains a current weather story with a corresponding image and information about each town. The town weather pages contain weather conditions and other town information or relevant articles/content. Images are used to reflect the current weather condition data on the town pages and the towns themselves.
