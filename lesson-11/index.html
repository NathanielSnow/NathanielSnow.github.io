Idaho’s historic weekend weather

by Maddie Capron

Snow hit parts of Idaho hard this weekend — yes, in June. The National Weather Service in Pocatello said it hasn’t issued a winter weather product in June in at least 15 years. “Winter in June is Snow much fun!” the Pocatello forecasters said on Twitter. “We haven’t issued a Winter Weather product in June in at least 15 years. And here it is!”
